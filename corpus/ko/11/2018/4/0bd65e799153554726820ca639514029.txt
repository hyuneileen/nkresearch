On the basis of relative neutralization capacity, its application amount equivalent to application amount of slaked limes is determined. The test of meliorating acid soil showed that the application amount of the weathered carbonate rocks equivalent to the application amount of slaked lime of 7t per jongbo provideds the optimal effect.
