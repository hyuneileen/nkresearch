The variation of geomagnetic field in our country calculated by IGRF12 in the period of 1995 to 2020 is nearly linear and absolute field components continue increasing. However, it is necessary to establish geomagnetic base observation to improve the correctness of geomagnetic field survey in the local parts of the country.
