A Method of Ultra-Wideband Impulse Measurement Using Loop Antenna Pak Ju Song, Jong Won Chol and Ri Yong Myong 주체107(2018)년 5월 5일 원고접수 In this paper, the theoretical model on radiation of ultra-wideband impulse was studied, and the radiated field measurement method of ultra-wideband impulse using loop antenna was established.
