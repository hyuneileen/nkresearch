[4] 刘晓燕 等; 种子, 24, 6, 18, 2015. 주체109(2020)년 4월 5일 원고접수 On Fruit Maturing Condition Affecting Seed Germinating of Cymbidium by Tissue Culture Pak Kum Song, Jo Sin Ok and Han Yong Gwang The proper condition for fruit maturing to get the best germinative ability of Cymbidium seeds for the purpose of more efficient germinating by tissue culture method was studied. When the maturing period of Cymbidium fruit is over 6 months the seeds have the ability to germinate and the ability is the best when it’s 9 months. The seeds have the ability to germinate when the accumulated temperature is over 3 400℃ during the fruit maturing, and when it’s 4 900～ 5 400℃ the germinating percentage is 100% and the germinating period is less than 60 days. Keywords: Cymbidium, fruit maturing, seed germinating, accumulated temperature
