The rational conditions to synthesize disodium hydrogen phosphate with high purity in great yield from 85% phosphoric acid and solid sodium carbonate are as follows: the reaction temperature is 60℃, the molar ratio of phosphoric acid and sodium carbonate is 1：1.02, the solid-liquid ratio is 1：3.15 and pH of reaction end point is 7.27～7.30.
