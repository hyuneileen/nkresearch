Effect of Silica Sol on the Strength of Cement Concrete 주체106(2017)년 10월 5일 원고접수 Han Hyok, Jo Yong Chol We assessed the effect of silica sol on the strength of cement concrete and found that silica sol reacted with calcium hydroxide to form calcium silicate hydrate and that as a result silica sol accelerated the hydration reaction of cement and increased the strength of cement concrete.
