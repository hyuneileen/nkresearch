[6] 赵丽丽 等; 催化学报, 30, 7, 637, 2009. 주체107(2018)년 1월 5일 원고접수 Reaction Characteristics of the Fe–K–Cu–Co–Si Catalyst for Fischer–Tropsch Synthesis Yun Chun Ae, Han Un Chol and Ri Chang Bong The reaction characteristics of the iron–based catalyst in the pressure fixed–bed Fischer– Tropsch synthesis(FTS) reactor was investigated. The cobalt component in the precipitated iron–based catalyst for FTS plays the promoter action that increases the production of the liquid paraffin. In the precipitated iron–based catalyst for FTS added the cobalt with a promoter, the production of the liquid paraffin is about 140g/m3, its selectivity is high and also the catalytic lifetime and the stability are good, therefore the catalyst has good prospects for industrial application.
