[4] Konghai Ni et al.; Journal of Chromatography, B 990, 181, 2015. 주체107(2018)년 4월 5일 원고접수 Determination of Octocrylene, Ethylhexylmethoxycinnamate, Butylmethoxydibenzoylmethane in Cosmetics by UPLC Method Yun Jong Ho, Kim Song Hui and Jon Hye Gyong We established the analytical method that could simply determine octocrylene, ethylhexylmethoxycinnamate and butylmethoxydibenzoylmethane in cosmetics by ultra performance liquid chromatography. The linear range of calibration curve of octocrylene, ethylhexylmethoxycinnamate, butylmethoxydibenzoylmethane is 0.1～50μg/mL, the detection limit(S/N=3) is below 0.1μg/mL, the variation coefficient of determination for octocrylene, ethylhexylmethoxycinnamate and butylmethoxydibenzoylmethane in various cosmetics is below 3.9% and the recovery percent is over 95.8%. Key words: UPLC, cosmetics
