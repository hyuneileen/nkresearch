Regeneration of Sludge during Phosphating Process and Its Use 주체107(2018)년 4월 5일 원고접수 Ri Jong Hyok, Yun Myong The principal components of sludge during phosphating process of steel surface are FePO4 and Zn3(PO4)2. The regeneration process of phosphating sludge consists of phosphoric acid dissolution, filtering separation, reduction by Zn metal and adjusting stage of acidity. It is found that in case of treating the steel product with the phosphate solution regenerated from phosphating sludge, no influence on its corrosion resistance is through the pitting corrosion test. Key words: phosphating, sludge, corrosion resistance
