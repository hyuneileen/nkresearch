The rational conditions for cerium separation by the precipitation method of double sulphate are as follows: the concentration of rare earth is 0.4mol/L in rare earth sulphate solution, the mass ratio of sodium sulphate and rare earth is 1.5, the concentration of fluorine ion is 0.2mol/L in precipitation solution, the concentration of sulfuric acid is 1mol/L in precipitation solution and the precipitation temperature is 70～90℃.
