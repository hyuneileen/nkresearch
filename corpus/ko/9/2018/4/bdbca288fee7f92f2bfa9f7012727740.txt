Dehumidification from Anisole by Using the Packed Dehydrating Column 주체107(2018)년 7월 5일 원고접수 Kim Sung Chol We suggested a model to remove the moisture from anisole by nitrogen gas and decreased the moisture content to 0.002% in anisole of which moisture content was 0.02% by using the packed dehydrating column. The operation conditions of the packed dehydrating column are as follows: the temperature is 293.15K, the diameter of column is 30mm, the height of packed layer is 1 100mm and the molar ratio of nitrogen and anisole is 6：1.
