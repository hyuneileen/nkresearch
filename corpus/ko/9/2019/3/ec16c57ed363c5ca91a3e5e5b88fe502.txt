5100PC》), 흐름주입분석체계(《PERKIN ELMER FIAS–400》), 자동시료주입기(《PERKIN ELMER AS–90》), 연무전극방전등(《PERKIN ELMER》), 전자분석천평(《AEG–120》)을, 시약으로는 1mg/mL Pb표준용액, 99.5% K3[Fe(CN)6](적혈염), 30% H2O2, 96% NaBH4, 36.5% HCl, In this method, the linear range of calibration curve is 0～20μg/L, the detection limit(3σ) is 0.013μg/L and the relative standard deviation is 0.37%(n=11, CPb=10μg/L).
