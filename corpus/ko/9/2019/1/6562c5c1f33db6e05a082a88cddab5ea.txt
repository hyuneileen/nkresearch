When the ion association complex is extracted with toluene, its fluorometric wavelength is 572.8nm(excitation at 532.8nm). The linear range of calibration curve is 0 ～ 0.12μg/mL. The quantitation limit and detection limit are 2.3, 1.7μg/L, respectively. Key words：arsenic(Ⅴ), rhodamine 6G, fluorometric determination
