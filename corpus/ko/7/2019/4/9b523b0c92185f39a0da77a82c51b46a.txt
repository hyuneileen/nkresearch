A Nonlinear Uzawa Algorithm for Generalized Saddle Point Problems Hwang Myong Gun, Pak Yong Song and Han Ryu Gyong 주체108(2019)년 6월 10일 원고접수 We propose a nonlinear algorithm for generalized saddle point problems to accelerate the convergence of the inexact Uzawa algorithm, and prove its convergence. Also, we show the effectiveness of the proposed method by a numerical example. Key words: saddle point problem, Uzawa algorithm
