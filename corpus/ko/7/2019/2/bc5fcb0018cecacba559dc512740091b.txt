Prime Spectrum of a Semimodule over a Non-commutative Semiring 주체107(2018)년 12월 5일 원고접수 Pae Won Sok, Han Song Chol We introduce the Zariski topology on the set of all the prime subsemimodules for a semimodule over a non-commutative semiring, and study the interplay between the topological properties such as separation, irreducibility and compactness and the algebraic properties of the semimodule. Key words: top semimodule, prime subsemimodule, Zariski topology
