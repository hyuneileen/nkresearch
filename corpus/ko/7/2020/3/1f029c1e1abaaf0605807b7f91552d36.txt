Wang et al.; IEJG, 10, 2, 37, 2017. 주체109(2020)년 3월 15일 원고접수 On a Ricci Quarter-Symmetric Conformal Metric Recurrent  Connection in an Einstein Manifold Yun Kum Song, Ho Tal Yun In this paper we newly defined a Ricci quarter-symmetric conformal metric recurrent connection that is conformaly equivalent to a Ricci quarter-symmetric in an Einstein manifold, and studied the volume flatness condition, conjugate symmetry condition and relation with the Levi-Civita connection and constant curvature condition. And we newly studied forms of the Ricci quarter-symmetric conformal metric recurrent connection satisfying the Schur’s theorem. Keywords: quarter-symmetric, metric recurrent connection, constant curvature, conjugate symmetry condition
