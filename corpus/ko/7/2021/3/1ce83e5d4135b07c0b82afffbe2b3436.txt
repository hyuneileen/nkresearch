Sharma; Discrete Math., 338, 576, 2015. 주체110(2021)년 3월 5일 원고접수 Repeated-Root Hermitian Self-Dual Negacyclic Codes of Length nm pl2 over a Finite Field Ryang Myong Guk, Kim Ryul In this paper, we construct all the simple-root Hermitian self-dual negacyclic codes of length ml2 over a finite field  and the repeated-root Hermitian self-dual negacyclic codes of length F of odd characteristic p and determine the number of such codes, where l is an odd prime nm pl2 2q different from p . Our study is based on a new observation about the complete set of representatives from distinct −2q cyclotomic cosets modulo ml4 . Keywords: finite field, cyclotomic coset, self-dual negacyclic code
