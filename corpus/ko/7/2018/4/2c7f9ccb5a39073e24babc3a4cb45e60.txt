Compressed Sensing via General-Dual-Based Analysis Lasso 주체107(2018)년 6월 5일 원고접수 Choe Chol Guk, Kim Song Yol This article considers compressed sensing of signals which are sparse or approximately sparse in terms of a highly overcomplete frame from undersampled data corrupted with additive noise. We show that the properly constrained 1l -analysis, called general-dual-based analysis Lasso, stably recovers a signal which is nearly sparse in terms of the frame. Key words: sparse signal recovery, frame, compressed sensing
