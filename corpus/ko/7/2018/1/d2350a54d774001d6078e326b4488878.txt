Compressed Sensing via General-Dual-based Analysis Dantzig Selector 주체106(2017)년 8월 10일 원고접수 Choe Chol Guk, Rim Myong Gil This article considers compressed sensing of signals that are sparse or approximately sparse in terms of a highly overcomplete frame from undersampled data corrupted with additive noise. We show that the properly constrained 1l -analysis, called general-dual-based analysis Dantzig Selector, stably recovers a signal which is nearly sparse in terms of a frame. Key words: sparse recovery, compressed sensing
