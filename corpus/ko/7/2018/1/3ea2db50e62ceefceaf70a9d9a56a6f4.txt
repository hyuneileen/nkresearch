Asgari; IAENG International Journal of Applied Mathematics, 45, 2, 14, 2015. 주체106(2017)년 8월 10일 원고접수 A Decomposition-Operational Matrix Method for a Kind of Fractional Mixed Volterra-Fredholm Integro-Differential Equations Choe Hui Chol, Jang Kyong Jun We study a decomposition-operational matrix method for a kind of fractional mixed Volterra-Fredholm integro-differential equations. Firstly, we give the decomposition scheme and the method for combining it with the operational matrix method. Secondly, we prove the existence and uniqueness of the approximate solution and present the error estimation formula. Key words: Caputo fractional derivative, fractional integro-differential equation, numerical method, operational matrix method
