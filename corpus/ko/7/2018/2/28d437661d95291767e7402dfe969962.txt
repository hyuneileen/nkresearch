Lett., 38, 1, 2010. 주체106(2017)년 12월 5일 원고접수 Approximate Calculation of the Bias Optimal Policy in Control of a Queue to Maximize the Average Reward Jon Yong Chol We consider a joint control problem of arrival and service rates of the M/M/1 queue to maximize the average reward. When the arrival and service rates take continuous values, we propose an approximate calculation algorithm of the bias optimal policy and prove the convergence of the algorithm.  Key words: queue, control, bias optimal policy
