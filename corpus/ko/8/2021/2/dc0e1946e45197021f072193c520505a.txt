Rev., B66, 174429, 2002. 주체109(2020)년 12월 5일 원고접수 Physical Properties of Fe-Based Full-Heusler  Thermoelectric Materials Jong Ju Yong, Hong Su Jong and Pak Su Il In this paper, the structural, phasic and mechanical stabilities as well as elastic, electronic and thermal properties of Fe-based full-Heusler thermoelectric materials, Fe2XY(X=Ti, Zr, Y=Si, Sn), have been successfully studied by using the first principles calculations based on density functional theory (DFT). The results show that Fe2XY(X=Ti, Zr, Y=Si, Sn) has not only a good thermoelectric properties but also mechanical and thermal properties, and these materials may be promising candidates for a next generation thermoelectric materials.
