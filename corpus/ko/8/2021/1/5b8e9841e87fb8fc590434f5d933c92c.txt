Electron., 26, 2614, 2015. 주체109(2020)년 9월 5일 원고접수 The Optical Properties of Mg2B2O5:Co2+-Pink Pigment Synthesized by Solution Combustion Method Han Chol Myong, Sin Chol Nam and Choe Nam Chol We have synthesized the pink pigment of Mg2B2O5:Co2+ by using solution combustion method with glycine base and obtained the chromaticity and optical bandgap experimentally. The first principle calculations are in good agreement with experimental results and show that the chromaticity and optical bandgap come from the d-orbitals of Co2+ ion.
