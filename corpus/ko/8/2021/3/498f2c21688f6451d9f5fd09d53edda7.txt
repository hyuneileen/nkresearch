On the Analysis of Radioactive Nuclides in Environmental Samples 주체110(2021)년 3월 5일 원고접수 Pak Chol Sun, Ri Ju Yon We proposed the method for determining the β radioactive nuclide in environmental samples and its radioactivity. We verified the distribution characteristics of β spectra according to the thickness of sample and each kind of nuclide, and determined the specific radioactivity with high accuracy.
