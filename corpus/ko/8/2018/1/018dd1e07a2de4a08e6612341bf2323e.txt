Power Plants Coolant Leak Detection by Acoustic Method 주체106(2017)년 9월 5일 원고접수 Kim Song Thaek, Ro Kwang Chol Based on the consideration of coolant leakage detection methods in NPP, we have proposed a real-time monitoring method of coolant leakage by acoustic method. We have evaluated the existance and location of leakage throughout spectrum analysis and correlation analysis of sound wave intensity level, and then, using decay law of sound wave according to distance, offerred a method that calculated sound power level in leakage point and determined leakage amount from it. Key words: coolant leakage, leakage detection, cross correlation
