Alzubadi, Duaa Majid Hameed; World Journal of Nuclear Science and Technology, 7, 67, 주체108(2019)년 12월 5일 원고접수 － 118 － 종합대학학보 물리학 주체109(2020)년 제66권 제2호 Calculation of Mean Square Radius of Some Light Nuclei with the  Generalized Hartree-Fock Approximation Method Kim Thae Song, Kim Ryon Hui and O Su Il We calculated the mean square radius of some light nuclei with the generalized Hartree-Fock approximation method and compared the results with DZ model results to assess the accuracy of the new method. We cleared that the generalized Hartree-Fock approximation method could relatively well explain the binding energies and structural properties of light nuclei except the magic nuclei.
