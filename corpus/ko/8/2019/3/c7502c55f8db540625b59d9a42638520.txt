From the experimental results, it is found that the constituent rate of CO to CO2 of produced gas can be increased by adding methyl alcohol in coal slurry. The more the additive amount is, the more the productive amount of gas is. As the coal content increases, at first, the gas generation slowly increases and then, it rapidly increases, finally, decreases.
