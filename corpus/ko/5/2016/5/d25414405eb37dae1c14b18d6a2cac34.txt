To keep the amplitude of output signal maximized, resonant frequency of primary circuit should be coincided to the secondary circuit. Moreover, the quality-factors of every circuit should be maintained to be maximized (over 50 percent), and the inductance of outgoing line must be less as possible than that of the coil.
