Reduction of EM Wave in Free Space-Plasma-Metal Plane System 주체105(2016)년 5월 5일 원고접수 Sim Yun Bo, Kim Kwang Il The reduction of electromagnetic wave in free space-plasma-metal plane system is theoretically discussed. Here the absorption of electromagnetic wave by interaction with plasma is evaluated. The obtained result can be applied to the propagation of the electromagnetic wave in highly inhomogenous plasma.
