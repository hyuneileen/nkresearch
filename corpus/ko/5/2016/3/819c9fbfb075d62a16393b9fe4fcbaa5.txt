As the result, we confirmed that the field distribution between both electrode was over electric field strength(30kV/cm) for the electric breakdown of air, so the aerial particles between both electrode can be ionized and charged with the quantity of charge over the certain value. We can collect the aerial particles of 0.3～1.0μm to the collection electrode with high efficiency.
