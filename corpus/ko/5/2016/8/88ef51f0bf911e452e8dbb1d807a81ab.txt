According to the results, they’re grouped into three types: IAG, CCG, POG and RRG. Until now, many people considered that Kwanmobong, Taedok, Rimmyongdong, Manthapsan intrusive units were included in Tanchon complex. We consider these intrusive units as ones formed at tectonic-magmatic stage that formed Hyesan complex. And we clarified that the most Mesozoic Amnokgang complex were constituted in RRG.
