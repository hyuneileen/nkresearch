Natural Gas Chemistry, 19, 161, 2010. 주체104(2015)년 9월 5일 원고접수 맺 는 말 참 고 문 헌 － 89 － 종합대학학보(자연과학)   주체105(2016)년  제62권  제1호 Synthesis of n-Butyric Acid by Electrolytic Oxidation of n-Buthyl Alcohol Choe Hyon Chol, Ri Yong Chol, Om Chol I and Kang Hyo Il We studied the electrolytic oxidation of n-buthyl alcohol by using PbO2/Pb anode, Pb cathode. When the electrolytic temperature is 40～45℃, the electrolyte is 5% H2SO4 and the current density is 20mA/cm2, the yield of n-butyric acid is over 70%.
