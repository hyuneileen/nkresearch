When MBC of 0.8g per 1kg was injected to the rat in a day for more than 30 days, there was no accumulation of MBC. In the same condition, there was no difference in general physiological indicators and biochemical ones of blood between the control group and the test group. Overall, this study suggests that MBC might safely serve in vivo.  Key words: MBC, toxicity, acute, subacute － 98 －
