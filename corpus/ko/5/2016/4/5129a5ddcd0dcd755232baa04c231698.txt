Mondal et al.; Journal of Physical Sciences, 12, 221, 2008. 주체104(2015)년 12월 5일 원고접수 Preparation of AZO Thin Film by SILAR Kim Myong Ho We discussed the spectroscopy of aluminum doped ZnO(AZO) thin film, the effect of some factors on the preparation of the film by SILAR, and the crystal structure of the prepared film. The AZO thin film has greater absorbance than the undoped ZnO thin film especially in the ultraviolet region of the spectrum. The Zn-O stretching band (400cm−1) appears when the samples are heated at 200 °C but a significant increase in the band intensity appears only at 300 °C. The optimal conditions for the preparation of the high quality AZO thin film with 189Ω/□ by SILAR are as follows: concentration of zinc acetate solution 0.1mol/L, concentration of sodium hydroxide 0.2mol/L, molar ratio of Zn to Al 100：1, solution pH 9, temperature of water bath 90℃, dipping time 5min, annealing temperature 673K. The prepared AZO thin film has the crystal structure with predominant c-axis orientation and c-axis length becomes short when Al was added into ZnO film. The particle size in the AZO film increases with increasing annealing temperature, which means the improvement of the quality of the film.
