Tortora; Principles of Anatomy ＆ Physiology, John Wiley ＆ Sons Inc., 703～706, 2012. 주체105(2016)년 6월 5일 원고접수 Influence of MS Preparation, MTS and Cylaboril on the Change of Histological Structure of Adrenal Gland of Rat Rim Ko Gun In this paper, the influence of MS preparation, MTS and cylaboril on the change of histological structure of the adrenal gland of rat was discussed. MS preparation increases more the mass of adrenal gland by 5.3, 10.7% compared with the MTS and cylaboril. MS preparation increases more the thickness of fasciculate zone of adrenal gland cortex by 2.2, 3.3%, the thickness of reticularis zone by 1.6, 3.7% compared with the MTS and cylaboril.
