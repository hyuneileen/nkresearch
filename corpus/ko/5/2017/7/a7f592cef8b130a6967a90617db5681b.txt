Inversion of the Resistivity Data for Dike in the Tunnel 주체106(2017)년 3월 5일 원고접수 Pak Un Gyong, Ri Il Gyong We proposed an inversion of resistivity data in tunnel using control method and verified its efficiency. First, we confirmed throughout model experiment that accuracy of solution of forward problem was enough guaranteed when the number of terms of infinite series of apparent resistivity formula to use for the inversion of resistivity data in the tunnel was taken to more than 20. And then we established the inversion of that and verified its reliability throughout model experiment. This method can be effectively used for interpretation of resistivity data in the tunnel.  Key words: mine direct current electric method, resistivity, inversion
