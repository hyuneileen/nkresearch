[2] Mehmat Topsakal et al.; Journal of Physical Chemistry, 117, 5943, 2013. 주체106(2017)년 3월 5일 원고접수 First Principles Study of Reduction Mechanism of  Graphene Oxide by Hydrazine Yu Chol Jun, Kim Su Jong and Pak Kum Song We have calculated a binding energy of epoxy group on graphene oxide by using first principles method, determined the reduction pathways of epoxy and hydroxyl groups by hydrazine and hydrazine-derivatives, and calculated their activation energies. It was concluded that the reduction by epoxy group of graphene oxide is more enhanced by hydrazine-derivatives dissociated in liquid than hydrazine gas.
