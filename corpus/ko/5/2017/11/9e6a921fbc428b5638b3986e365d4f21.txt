Optimal Boundary in Binomial Tree Model for American Put Option 주체106(2017)년 7월 5일 원고접수 O Hyong Chol, Jon Il Gwang We study the existence and monotonicity of optimal boundary in binomial tree model for American put option with time dependent coefficients. We prove the existence of optimal boundary for American put option under some monotonicity assumptions on the interest rate, dividend rate and volatility. Key words: American put option, binomial tree model
