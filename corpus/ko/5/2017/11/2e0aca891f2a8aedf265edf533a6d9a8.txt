Quantum Elect., 28, 205, 1992. 주체106(2017)년 7월 5일 원고접수 Active Coupling between Plasmonic Waveguides  via in-between Gain-Assisted Nanoresonator Ho Kum Song, Han Yong Ha and Im Song Jin We studied the active coupling between plasmonic waveguides via in-between gain-assisted nanoresonator. Precise control of phase difference between coupled and transmitted surface plasmon polaritons can be performed by manipulating pumping rate at an appropriate frequency detuning. This coupling can be used in the control of light at nanoscale such as nano-optical logic gate, directional coupler, switching and so on. Key words: surface plasmon polariton, waveguides, coupled resonators, phase modulator
