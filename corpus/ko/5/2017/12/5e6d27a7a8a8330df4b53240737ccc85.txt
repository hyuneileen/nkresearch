Ghamsari; Journal of Composites Materials, 49, 27, 2015. 주체106(2017)년 8월 5일 원고접수 Displacement Analysis of Asymmetrical Cross-Ply Elastic Laminated Composite Plate Subjected to In-Plane Force and External Force Song Song Gwan, Ri Un Gyong We analysed the displacement of a fiber reinforced asymmetrical cross-ply laminated composite on elastic foundation, subjected to in-plane force and external force. We derived the displacement equation of asymmetric cross-ply laminates composite plate and considered the method of displacement analysis. The validation is demonstrated by numerical examples. Key words: cross-ply laminate, asymmetrical laminate, displacement equation
