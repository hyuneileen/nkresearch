Henkenseier et al.; Journal of Membrane Science, 447, 228, 2014. 주체106(2017)년 8월 5일 원고접수 Influence of Some Factors on Sulfonation of Poly(tetrafluoroethylene-co- hexafluoropropylene)-Styrene System Graft Copolymer Membrane Kim Yong Chol, Ri Song Bom We considered the influence of some factors on sulfonation of poly(tetrafluoroethylene-co- hexafluoropropylene)-styrene(FEP-St) system graft copolymer membrane. When FEP-St system graft copolymer membrane whose degree of graft is (35±3)% is treated with 1, 1, 2, 2-tetrachloroethane solution of 5% chlorosulfonic acid at 30℃ for more than 9 hours, the degree of sulfonation becomes 98%.
