Chem., 25, 6, 664, 2000. 주체106(2017)년 6월 5일 원고접수 Synthesis of Vanadyl–Cysteinate Coordination Compound and Its Characteristics Ri Uk Ryong, Ri Hyok Su and Ri Pun We newly synthesized vanadyl-cysteinate coordination compound with the composition of Na2[VO(Cys)2(H2O)]·H2O and established the synthetic method. Putting vanadyl sulphate and cysteine hydrochloride in the aqueous solution of pH 7 at 10℃, adding acetone, we got vanadyl-cysteinate coordination compound as the violet precipitate. Vanadyl-cysteinate coordination compound has distorted six-coordinated octahedral structure of VO(N2S2O) type where N of amino group, S of thiol group of Cys2－ and O of water are in form of six-membered ring coordinated to VO2＋ separately.
