Phys., 193, 1359, 1992. 주체105(2016)년 10월 5일 원고접수 － 94 － 종합대학학보(자연과학)   주체106(2017)년  제63권  제2호 On the Deactivation Kinetics of Copolymerization of Ethene and Butene-1 by Metallocene Catalyst Jang Un Bom, Maeng Thae Won We investigated the kinetics of copolymerization of ethene and buthene-1 in the metallocene/methylaluminoxan(MAO) system under the conditions of low pressure, temperature and suspension. The deactivation follows first-order reaction irrelatively with the concentration of catalyst. The activation energy and thermal stability depend on the ligand structure, the activation energy and half-life of catalyst II and III are higher than that of catalyst I. Key words: deactivation, half-life, kinetics, metallocene catalyst
