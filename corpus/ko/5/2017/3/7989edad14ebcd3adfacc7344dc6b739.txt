Res., 210, 158, 2011. 주체105(2016)년 11월 5일 원고접수 On a Property of Efficient Solution in Multiobjective  Linear Programming Ri Hong Il, Pak Yong Song We consider a property of the efficient solution in the multiobjective linear programming. We analyze a dependence of row vectors of the coefficient matrix of the objective functions in multiobjective linear programming and propose a new necessary and sufficient condition to be the efficient solution based on it. Key words: multiobjective linear programming, efficient solution, coefficient matrix
