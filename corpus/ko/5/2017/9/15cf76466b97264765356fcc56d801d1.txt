Previous method, estimating the paleo-stress state with the attitude of veins, has some disadvantages such as the hardness to explore the correct answer and the large amount of calculation. I suggested a method to enhance the correct of answer and the rate of calculation, preselecting the initial value of principle stress axis based on the attitude of veins.
