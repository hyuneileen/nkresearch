Investigation on the 4-Channel Boron Concentration Measurement Mun Chung Nam, Ryang Pong Man and Jo In Hyong 주체106(2017)년 5월 5일 원고접수 We found the measurement accuracy got much higher as compared with single channel boron concentration measurement equipment if 4-channel boron concentration measurement equipment was used.
