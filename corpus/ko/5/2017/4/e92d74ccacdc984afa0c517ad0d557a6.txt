Modeling and Control of Hydraulic Servo System of One-Legged Robot 주체105(2016)년 12월 5일 원고접수 Yun Chang Jin This paper deals with modeling and control of hydraulic system of the one-legged robot. In this study damping parameter for modeling the rotational motion of one-legged robot with hydraulic drives and the control parameters are determined by combination of the simulation on the model with identified parameters and experiment. Satisfactory coincidence of simulation and experiment, good response of controlled system indicate that proposed identification method and simulation model are reliable, and control system has good performance. Key words: one-legged robot, electro-hydraulic servo system
