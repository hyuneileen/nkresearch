To overcome the question that depends much on expert’s subjective judgment in traditional risk assessment, this paper structure the fuzzy comprehensive evaluation model of quality risk assessment based on AHP, then combine expert’s subjective judgement with objective quantitative analysis. By using this method, quality risk assessment can be objectivized. Key words: AHP, fuzzy comprehensive evaluation, quality risk assessment
