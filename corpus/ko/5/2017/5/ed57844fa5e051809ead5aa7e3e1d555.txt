Newton et al.; Sensors and Actuators, A 109, 180, 2004. 주체106(2017)년 1월 5일 원고접수 The Alcohol Sensing Properties of ZnO(CeO2, Sb2O5) Thin-Film Ri Chun Guk, Ri Jong Nam and Kwon Chol Ho ZnO(CeO2, Sb2O5)-based thin-film gas sensors with different Ce/Zn ratios has been fabricated and their gas sensing properties to alcohol, propane and acetone have been tested. ZnO-based thin films with thickness of about 8 mμ consist of almost spherical particles with size ranging from 25～50nm. The gas sensing properties of ZnO(Sb2O5) based thin films depend on the concentration of dopant. The sensitivities of 1at.% CeO2-ZnO and 4at.% CeO2-ZnO films are higher than that of pure ZnO and 7at.% CeO2-ZnO. Key words: alcohol, ZnO, CeO2
