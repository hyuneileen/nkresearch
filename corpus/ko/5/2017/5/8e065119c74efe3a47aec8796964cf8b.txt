CFD Simulation of Volcanic Gases Flow in Volcanic Conduits 주체106(2017)년 1월 5일 원고접수 Kim Yon Ho, An Chol Ho We studied the possibility of CFD simulation for the flow process of volcanic gases in volcanic conduits of the geothermal system beneath of the “ㅂ” volcanic area. The research results revealed that the flow of volcanic gases would not considerably influence pressure and thermal fields around volcanic conduits and the flow rate was not fast enough to form the local thermal fields. Key words: volcanic conduit, CFD simulation
