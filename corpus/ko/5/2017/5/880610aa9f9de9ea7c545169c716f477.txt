Synthesis of Sodium 3, 4-Dimethoxyphenylglycidate 주체106(2017)년 1월 5일 원고접수 Kim Mi Hye, Ju Yun Hui We studied the method for synthesis of sodium 3, 4-dimethoxyphenylglycidate from veratraldehyde and ethyl chloroacetate. The reasonable conditions of this reaction are as follows: the molar ratio of ethyl chloroacetate and veratraldehyde is 1.4, the molar ratio of veratraldehyde and catalyst is 1.2 and the reaction temperature is 20℃.
