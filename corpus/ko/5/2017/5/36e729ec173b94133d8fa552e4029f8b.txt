Calc., 5, 2, 121, 2014. 주체106(2017)년 1월 5일 원고접수 Uniqueness and Existence of the Solution for a Generalized  Multi-Point Boundary Value Problem of Multi-Term  Fractional Ordinary Differential Equation Choe Hui Chol, Jong Kum Song We proposed a generalized fractional multi-point boundary value problem which had a weaker limitation than previous works and studied the existence and uniqueness of the solution for this problem. For this we defined new solution space, proved that this space was a Banach space in respect to its norm and gave a sufficient condition that the solution of integral equation equivalent to main problem existed uniquely.
