The molecular weight of purified extracellular recombinant phytase is determined to be 45kDa from SDS-PAGE. It displays the maximal activity at pH 4.5 and remains stable in the broad range of pH 3.0 ～ 10.0 at 40 ～ 50 ℃ . The optimum temperature of extracellular recombinant phytase is 55℃ at reaction time of 15min. The Km and Vmax for sodium phytate are 0.361mmol/L and 1 314μmol/(min·mg), respectively.
