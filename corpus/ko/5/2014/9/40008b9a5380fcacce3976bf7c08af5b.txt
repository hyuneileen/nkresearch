Particle Size and Hydrophily of White Earth in Taedong Area 주체103(2014)년 5월 5일 원고접수 Pak Hye Suk As a result of determination by a sieve analysis method, the white earth with less than 0.1mm in size is more than 48 percent and particle size of white earth is average 10μm. Density of white earth is 1.78～1.90g/cm3 in natural, 2.68～2.72g/cm3 in handpicking. This shows that the white earth in Taedong area can be used by handpicking in nature not by reducing to powder specially. Moistrue adsorptive saturation amount of white earth is average 32.9mg and water absorptive amount is 2.3%, differ little from Sammyo-ri’s. Its thermal loss of weight is average 6.3% due to water of OH－ type. Key words: white earth, particle size, hydrophily － 142 －
