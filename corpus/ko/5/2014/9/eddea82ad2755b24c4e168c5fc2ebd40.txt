A new structure is designed here that ensures the stability of the flatcar moving on curved slope railway, while applying a new pulling method by a winch with four flatcars. COSMOS/Works, the finite element analysis program, is used to appropriate the strength of the assembly frame of the wheels and to determine the applying condition of melting strength.
