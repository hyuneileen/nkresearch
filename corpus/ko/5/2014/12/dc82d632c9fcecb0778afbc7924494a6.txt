In this method, a linear range of calibration curve was 0. 2～25μg/L, a detection limit was 0.068μg/L and a relative standard deviation (RSD) was 3.6%(1.5μg/L, n=11). The accuracy was examined throughout the arsenic measurement of standard sample GBW09101(human hair) and determination of trace arsenic in natural water and foods samples.
