In the oilflax-soybean intercropping, the yield of oilflax can be more than 1.4t/ha and the yield of soybean can be more than 1.8t/ha when the soybean variety is “Sunchon No. 3”, the oilflax’s sowing width is 30cm sulcus at 70cm ridge, the planting number of soybean is 30 per phyong and the fertilizer level is 30kg/ha for nitrogen.
