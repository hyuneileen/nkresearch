Geist et al.; Geophysical Research Letters, 35, 26, 2008. 주체103(2014)년 7월 5일 원고접수 Analysis Method of the Propagation Process of Tsunami by Boussinesq Model Pak Chun Ok, Kim Chang Gon We performed the analysis of the propagation process of tsunami along the coast of Korean East Sea. We simulated the propagation process of tsunami with linear Boussinesq model. This model is known as the model possible to analysis not only the height of tsunami but also its shape and generation of coastal current. When we use staggered explicit leap-frog finite-difference scheme and compare with observation data at 12 points, it is more correct than energy balance method.
