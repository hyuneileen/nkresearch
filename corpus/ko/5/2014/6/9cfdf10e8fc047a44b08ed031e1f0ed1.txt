We have verified throughout model experiment that our joint inversion has wide radius of convergence and little polyvalence than individual inversions of magnetic anomaly or self-spontaneous anomaly. Our joint inversion can be effectively used in simultaneously interpreting magnetic and self-spontaneous anomalies measured over 2D dike and clarifying the parameters.
