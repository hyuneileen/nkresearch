We determined the amount of outflow from upper lock by using the mass balance equation. In this paper, we studied how much water of “ㅁ” lock we need to dilute the downstream water of “ㅍ” city to satisfy 3rd degree, the state water quality evaluation degree. The study result shows that the amount of dilution water to improve the water quality depends on the water quality guideline level, the change character of each water quality index and the concentration of each water quality index.
