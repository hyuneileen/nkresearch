Influence of Excess Boron on Crystal Growth of cBN 주체103(2014)년 4월 5일 원고접수 Kim Hun, Ri Yong Sop We have studied the influence of excess boron on crystal growth of cBN. If cBN synthesis is processed in the system of excess boron, octahedral shaped crystals are obtained with high yield of cBN. And in the other case tetrahedral shaped crystals are obtained with low yield of cBN.  Key words: cBN, crystal growth － 37 －
