We proposed similarity measure based on weighted Euclidian distance and calculated the weights of parameter considering the importance of parameters on the classification of traffic state. And we studied a method of traffic state classification considering the weights of parameter. The experiment result shows that W-FCM method is more efficient than FCM method in the traffic state classification.
