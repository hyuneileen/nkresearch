[7] 肖啸庵 等; 同位素分离, 原子出版社, 203, 1999. 주체104(2015)년 1월 5일 원고접수 A New Method for Calculating the Mean Local Surface Reaction Rate of the Mass Transfer Process in the Countercurrent Two Phase Liquid System Kim Jung Man, Han Kyong Chan We suggested a new method calculating the reaction rate in the heterogeneous system of countercurrent two phase liquid. This method is based on the solution of the four components convection diffusion equation in the countercurrent two phase liquid system. The reaction rate can be described in the form of the mean local surface reaction rate which averaged the local surface reaction rate in a small zone including the given point.
