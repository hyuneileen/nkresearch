Removal of Some Pollution Materials in Sewage by Radioactive Ray 주체103(2014)년 11월 5일 원고접수 Ri Thong Il, Ri Song Bom We studied the sewage purification effect when the sewage samples of the primary settling pond of municipal purification plant were treated by radioactive ray. When we irradiated the sewage sample with radiation, COD of sewage sample was decreased to 40% at absorption dose of 10kGy and BOD was decreased to 30% at absorption dose of 5kGy. 4NH in sewage sample was removed 25% at absorption dose of 10kGy and Cl－ was removed about 25% at absorption dose of 5kGy. + Key words: sewage purification, COD, BOD － 49 －
