An Kwang Il, Ryu Ki Song We studied separating and identifying flavonid components in the peduncles of Hovenia dulcis. In the ethyl acetate fractions, TLC was done, substances of which Rf values were 0.75, 0.66 and 0.52 respectively were obtained, their spectra were measured by UV, IR, MS and 1H- and 13C-NMR and flavonoids, namely quercetin, taxifolin and myricetin were identified.
