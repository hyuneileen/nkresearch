At the power station, when surface resistivity is high, it is effective to find area with low resistivity such as tectonic crack and aquifer, and to make up ground system there. If we apply resistivity CT method, we can explicate effectively position, size, laying station and resistivity of the tectonic crack and aquifer with low resistivity.
