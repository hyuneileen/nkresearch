Wetland Flora in Sinsaeng Reservoir and Its Utilization Measures 주체104(2015)년 3월 5일 원고접수 Kim Hui Chol, Choe Myong Gil We surveyed the natural flora in Sinsaeng reservoir to divide a grassland type and clarify species organization and productivity of fodder grasses and harmful plants. There are 3 species of bad plants in this area, can be taken off effectively using the mixture of herbicide and powder soap. Through the above surveys and examinations, we concluded that the wetland in Sinsaeng reservoir can be improved and used as natural grassland by removing bad plants and additional sowing fodder grasses without its reclamation.
