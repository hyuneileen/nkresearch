Chem., 45, 20, 4549, 2002. 주체104(2015)년 3월 5일 원고접수 The Effects of Antioxidant Enzyme Mimic-EMC with Low Molecular Weight on Survival Ratio and Peripheral Blood Phase of Irradiated Animal Choe Su Ryon, Ri Hyong Gwan and Kang Jong Gyu This study illustrates the protective effects of EMC, which is a sort of salen-Mn complex compound with various antioxidant activities such as SOD, CAT, POD on the cellular damage accompanying the irradiation. When EMC is orally injected before and after irradiation, not only the survival ratio and life span in 30 days but also the recovery ratio of leucocyte and lymphocyte of rats goes up significantly compared with the control groups.
