Synthesis of 3′-Dietylamino-7′, 8′-Benzofluoran 주체104(2015)년 3월 5일 원고접수 Om Chol I, Pak Chol Ju We established a method for preparing of 3′-dietylamino-7′, 8′-benzofluoran(DEABF) from 2-(4′-diethylamino-2′-hydroxybenzoyl)benzoic acid(DEAHBB) and β -naphthol. The yield of DEABF is the highest (80%) when the molar ratio (DEAHBB：β-naphthol) is 1：1.3, the reaction temperature 20℃, and reaction time 24h.
