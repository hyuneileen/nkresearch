The reasonable synthesis conditions of 2-(4'-diethylamino-2'-hydroxybenzoyl) benzoic acid are the molar ratio of m-diethylaminophenol and phthalic anhydride 1：2, the reaction temperature 105～110℃ and the reaction time 12h and the yield of DHBB is 71%.
